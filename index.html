About me

Software Developer mastering the modern web stack. Leveraging a C++/C# background to build with HTML, CSS, JS, and React. I am a developer with a passion for logic and problem-solving, focused on building digital experiences that are not only functional and efficient but also intuitive and user-friendly.

My background in robust, object-oriented languages like C++ and C# has provided me with a strong foundation in software engineering principles, data structures, and the importance of clean, scalable code. I am now channeling that discipline and structured thinking to master the modern web development ecosystem. I see the transition to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript not as a change, but as an expansion: the opportunity to apply the same technical rigor to create dynamic and engaging user interfaces.

What drives me most is the full lifecycle of creation: from conceiving an idea to its final implementation, polishing every detail along the way. I am committed to continuous learning and eager to contribute to challenging projects where I can apply my skills and grow alongside a talented team. My goal is clear: to become a high-impact Full-Stack developer, and mastering the Frontend is my first strategic step on that path.
